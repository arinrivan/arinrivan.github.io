About

Arin Rivan exists to notice what usually passes unspoken. The small shifts in how we live, work, connect and disconnect — especially as technology accelerates faster than our ability to make sense of it.

These pages hold essays, fragments and quiet experiments in thinking. Nothing here is urgent. You are invited to read slowly.
